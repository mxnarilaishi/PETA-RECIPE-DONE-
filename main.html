My TOP 3 Favorite Foods

Welcome to My Food Blog!

These are the top 3 favorite foods of mine! I love them so much! I like to eat shrimp sinigang with a more sour taste, I like to eat bopis more if it's made by my mom, and I like to eat crispy tempura when it's served hot and fresh.

Top 1: Shrimp Sinigang

A comforting Filipino sour soup with fresh shrimp and vegetables.

Top 2: Spicy Bopis

A rich, savory dish packed with bold flavors and a spicy kick.

Top 3: Crispy Tempura

Light, crunchy, and perfect when served hot with a dipping sauce.
