Crispy Tempura

A simple and delicious Japanese-style fried dish with a light, crunchy batter.

20-25 minutes cooking time

Serves 4

INGREDIENTS

  • Tempura batter mix 1 cup
  • Ice-cold water 1/2 cup
  • Egg 1
  • Shrimp 8-10 pieces, peeled and deveined
  • Sweet potato 1 medium, sliced
  • Eggplant 1 small, sliced
  • Cooking oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Tentsuyu sauce for serving

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare the ingredients
    Slice the vegetables and pat the shrimp dry.
  2. Make the batter
    In a bowl, mix the tempura batter mix, egg, and ice-cold water until just combined. A few lumps are fine.
  3. Heat the oil
    Heat cooking oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  4. Coat and fry
    Dip the shrimp and vegetables into the batter and fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Drain and season
    Remove from the oil, drain on paper towels, and sprinkle with a little salt.
  6. Serve
    Serve hot with tentsuyu sauce or soy sauce with a squeeze of lemon.

About

Tempura is a popular Japanese fried dish known for its light, crisp batter and deliciously crunchy texture.
