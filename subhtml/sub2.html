Spicy Bopis

A rich and spicy Filipino dish made with ground pork, vegetables, and bold seasonings.

30-40 minutes cooking time

Serves 4-6

INGREDIENTS

  • Ground pork 500g
  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced
  • Onion 1 medium, chopped
  • Tomatoes 2 medium, diced
  • Ginger 1 tablespoon, minced
  • Bell pepper 1 medium, diced
  • Green chilies 2-3 pieces, sliced
  • Soy sauce 3 tablespoons
  • Vinegar 2 tablespoons
  • Oyster sauce 2 tablespoons
  • Brown sugar 1 tablespoon
  • Cooking oil 2 tablespoons
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Calamansi or lemon for serving

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Saute aromatics
    Heat oil in a pan. Saute garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Cook the pork
    Add the ground pork and cook until lightly browned and no longer pink.
  3. Add the vegetables
    Stir in tomatoes, bell pepper, and chilies. Cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  4. Season the bopis
    Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, oyster sauce, and brown sugar. Stir well and simmer for 8-10 minutes.
  5. Adjust flavor
    Season with salt and pepper. Add a squeeze of calamansi or lemon for a brighter taste.
  6. Serve
    Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy.

About

Spicy bopis is a beloved Filipino dish known for its bold flavors, rich pork base, and a slightly sweet, tangy, and spicy finish.
